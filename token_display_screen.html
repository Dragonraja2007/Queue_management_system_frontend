System Workflow

Our **Sensor-Based Hybrid Queue Management System** eliminates the need for camera tracking or physical queues. By combining digital ticketing with real-time physical counter presence detection, it guarantees fair, high-precision waiting calculations.

confirmation_number 1. Joining & Ticketing

01
System Initialization

Loads counter status, waiting queues, and historical average service times.

02
Home Access

Displays average wait times, active counters, and currently serving token to incoming visitors.

03
Counter Selection

Highlights the recommended counter with the shortest waiting time. Customer inputs name & phone.

04
Token Generation

Generates a unique digital ticket, saves the transaction, and returns ETA and position.

05
Virtual Queueing

Allows remote waiting via QR codes or Web. Eliminates standing in physical lines.

sensors 2. Sensor & Analytics

06
IR Sensor Detection

IR/Proximity sensor detects customer arrival at the desk and starts the service timer.

07
Service Completion

Sensor detects customer departure, stopping the service timer and logging the duration.

08
Self-Learning Predictor

Calculates and updates average counter service duration based on historical session times.

09
Predictive Wait Times

Uses the updated moving average and remaining queue length to update customer wait predictions.

10
Staff Dashboard Calling

Staff calls next customer, updating current serving token and live tracking screens.

check_circle
11. Token Completion

Queue count decreases, and new predictions are dynamically pushed to tracking screens.

12. System Output

Zero wait uncertainty, optimized counter distribution, and zero camera intrusion.
