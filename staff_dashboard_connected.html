System Workflow

Our **Sensor-Based Hybrid Queue Management System** eliminates the need for camera tracking or physical queues. By combining digital ticketing with real-time physical counter presence detection, it guarantees fair, high-precision waiting calculations.

confirmation_number 1. Joining & Ticketing

01
System Initialization

Loads counter status, waiting queues, and historical average service times.

02
Home Access

Displays average wait times, active counters, and currently serving token to incoming visitors.

03
Counter Selection

Highlights the recommended counter with the shortest waiting time. Customer inputs name & phone.

04
Token Generation

Generates a unique digital ticket, saves the transaction, and returns ETA and position.

05
Virtual Queueing

Allows remote waiting via QR codes or Web. Eliminates standing in physical lines.
